Enrollment

Start your journey toward academic excellence with Tuition Point Education Center.

Our Enrollment Process

Follow these simple steps to enroll in our educational programs.

1

Inquiry

Contact us via phone, email, or our website to express your interest and ask any initial questions about our programs.

2

Assessment

We conduct a brief academic assessment to understand the student's current level, strengths, and areas for improvement.

3

Consultation

Meet with our academic team to discuss the assessment results and create a personalized learning plan.

4

Enrollment

Complete the registration process, choose your schedule, and begin your educational journey with us.

Required Documents

Please prepare the following documents to complete the enrollment process.

For Students

  • Completed enrollment form (provided during consultation)
  • Copy of student's passport or ID
  • Recent passport-sized photograph
  • Most recent school report card or transcript

For Parents/Guardians

  • Copy of parent's/guardian's ID or passport
  • Proof of residence in Qatar
  • Emergency contact information
  • Signed terms and conditions form

Enrollment FAQs

Find answers to common enrollment questions.

When can I enroll my child?

We accept enrollments throughout the year, but it's recommended to enroll early to secure preferred time slots and maximize learning benefits.

Can I change subjects after enrollment?

Yes, you can modify your subject selection based on your child's needs. We recommend discussing any changes with our academic team to ensure continuity in learning.

What if my child has special learning needs?

We work with students of various learning abilities and can provide accommodations where possible. Please discuss your child's specific needs during the consultation phase.

What is your refund policy?

Our refund policy varies based on the program and timing of the withdrawal request. Complete details are provided in our terms and conditions document, which will be shared during enrollment.

Do you offer make-up classes for missed sessions?

We offer make-up classes for sessions missed due to illness or emergency, subject to availability and advance notice. Please refer to our attendance policy for details.
